Weicheng WANG

Biography

I am currently a post-doctoral fellow in the CSE department in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I obtained my PhD degree in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing WONG in 2023. During my Ph.D. period, I also conducted a research visit at the The University of Michigan (U-M), supervised by Prof. H. V. Jagadish in 2022-2023. I obtained my BEng degree in Information Science and Technology from Beijing Normal University in 2018.
