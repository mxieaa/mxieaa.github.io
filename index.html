Biography

I am currently a resercher in Shenzhen Institute of Computing Sciences. I obtained my PhD degree in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing WONG in 2019. I obtained my BEng degree in a joint program of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) and Sun Yat-sen University in 2014.
